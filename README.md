## 2) Information we collect
Depending on features you use, we may collect:

## 3) Camera, photos, and scanning
VitaSnap AI uses the camera to scan vitamins and food for nutrition insights.
- We do not access your photo library unless you grant permission and choose to import an image.
- **Image handling (choose and edit):**
  - **Option 1:** Images are processed on-device where possible; if cloud processing is required, images are transmitted securely and deleted after processing.
  - **Option 2:** Images may be uploaded securely for AI processing and retained only as needed to provide the feature, improve reliability, and prevent abuse, then deleted or de-identified.
